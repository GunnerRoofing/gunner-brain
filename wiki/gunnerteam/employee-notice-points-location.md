As part of your role at Gunner Roofing, you will use the GunnerTeam mobile app. This notice explains two features of the app that involve your work activity and your device location, so you know what to expect. Please read it and sign the acknowledgment at the end.

## 1. Recognition Points & Leaderboard

The app includes a recognition program that awards points for everyday work activities and lets you unlock achievement badges. Activities that earn points include:

- Adding job photos, completing checklists, and completing tasks
- Submitting change orders and passing/arriving on time for inspections
- Customer reviews associated with your work

A weekly leaderboard shows how teammates compare. All team members take part in the leaderboard. Entries are displayed by first name and last initial â€” this is a friendly internal scoreboard, not anonymous data, so coworkers may be able to recognize one another.

Points may become redeemable for rewards. Any reward with a cash value (for example, a gift card) is treated as taxable wages and is processed through payroll with applicable withholding.

**You may ask to pause your participation in the leaderboard at any time â€” including during a leave of absence, as a workplace accommodation, or for any personal reason â€” by contacting HR. You do not need to provide a reason.**

## 2. Location & Check-In

The app uses your device location to support job check-in and check-out and to estimate travel mileage between job sites. Depending on app settings, this may include background location while you are on the clock or traveling for work.

- **Purpose:** timekeeping, mileage estimation, and associating your work with the correct job.
- Location is collected for these work purposes and is not intended to monitor you outside of working hours. *[HR/IT to confirm the exact on-the-clock vs. off-the-clock behavior before distribution.]*
- Questions about what is collected and when can be directed to HR or IT at any time.
