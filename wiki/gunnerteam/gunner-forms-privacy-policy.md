# GunnerForms Privacy Policy

**Document ID:** IT-POL-PRIV-001 v1.0  
**Classification:** PUBLIC  
**Approved by:** Tyler Suffern + [[entities/Eric Recchia|Eric Recchia]]  
**Established:** 2026-04-06  
**Review cycle:** Annual / on change

Public-facing privacy policy for the GunnerForms iOS app. Required for Apple App Store Connect submission.

## Key Points

- **App collects zero data** — no PII, no analytics, no crash reports, no location, no contacts, no camera/mic
- **Form submissions** go directly to Monday.com — governed by Monday.com's privacy policy and Gunner's workspace config; Gunner operates no intermediate servers
- **Authentication** is handled by Monday.com + Google SSO — the app does not store, log, or transmit credentials
- **Session cookies** from Monday.com are stored locally in the WebView cookie store only, used solely to maintain the authenticated session

## Scope

Distributed exclusively through Apple Business Manager to Hexnode-managed iOS devices issued by Gunner Roofing. Not publicly available.
