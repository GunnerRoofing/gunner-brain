# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Document ID:** IT-POL-AUP-001  
**Version:** v1.1 (reformatted 2026-03-26)  
**Classification:** INTERNAL  
**Established:** 2026-03-18  
**Next Review:** 2027-03-18  
**Acknowledgment Required:** Yes — see AUP Acknowledgment Form (IT-POL-AUP-001)

---

## Purpose & Scope

Applies to all employees and contractors issued a Gunner Roofing company device (MacBook or iPhone). By using a company device, users agree to these terms. Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination.

---

## 1. Device Ownership & Monitoring

- All MacBooks and iPhones are **company property** — employees are custodians, not owners
- All devices are enrolled in Hexnode MDM — IT can remotely configure, monitor, update, and wipe
- **No expectation of privacy** on company devices
- Gunner may inspect device content, app usage, and network activity at any time without notice
- **Personal data risk:** Do not store personal photos, files, or data on company devices — they may be wiped without notice

---

## 2. The Gunner Tech Stack

All business operations must use approved applications only. See [[gunner/app-inventory]] for the full approved app list.

Prohibited: Installing unapproved apps, using personal cloud storage for work data, or circumventing MDM controls.

---

## 3. Password Management

- All work account passwords must be stored in **Keeper**
- Passwords must be unique and randomly generated — no reuse
- Personal passwords must NOT be stored in Keeper
- Employees must memorize their Keeper Master Password (IT cannot reset it)

---

## 4. Acceptable & Unacceptable Use

### Acceptable
- Using approved apps for business purposes
- Communicating with customers and colleagues via approved tools
- Accessing company systems for work tasks

### Unacceptable
- Using company devices for personal business or entertainment
- Installing unapproved apps
- Bypassing MDM controls or security policies
- Sharing device credentials with others
- Storing personal data on company devices

---

## 5. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Tools

### Approved AI Access
- Access to AI tools is provisioned by IT on a case-by-case basis
- Approved tools are managed and configured to meet Gunner's security standards

### What You Must Never Do
- Input customer data, financial information, project details, or any confidential information into any AI tool
- Use personal AI accounts (ChatGPT, etc.) on company devices for work purposes

### Personal AI Accounts
- Personal AI accounts on company devices are prohibited

---

## 6. Data Privacy & Customer Information

- Customer data must remain within approved Gunner systems (HubSpot, Monday, CompanyCam, etc.)
- Do not share customer information via personal email, personal messaging apps, or unauthorized cloud services
- All customer communications must occur via approved channels (Dialpad, Gmail, Google Chat)

---

## 7. Field Safety

- Do not use company devices while operating a vehicle
- Company devices must be secured when not in use in the field
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT immediately — do not wait

---

## 8. Security & Incident Reporting

Report immediately to IT:
- Lost or stolen device
- Suspicious email or phishing attempt
- Unexpected device behavior
- Any suspected security breach

Do not attempt to remediate a security incident alone — contact IT first.

---

## 9. Professionalism & Communications

- All communications via company devices are subject to review
- Maintain professional standards in all digital communications
- Company email and messaging may be reviewed for compliance purposes

---

## 10. Separation & Device Return

On employment separation:
- All company devices must be returned to IT immediately
- Data on returned devices will be wiped
- Failure to return devices may result in legal action
- IT will revoke all account access on the last day of employment

---

## 11. Accountability & Enforcement

- Policy acknowledged via signature at onboarding
- Violations subject to disciplinary action up to termination
- Policy reviewed annually; updates supersede prior versions
