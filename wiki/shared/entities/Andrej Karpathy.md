# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher. Originator of the LLM Wiki pattern — the approach this vault is built on. Former Director of AI at Tesla, founding member of OpenAI.

The LLM Wiki pattern: use an LLM to maintain a persistent, compounding knowledge base instead of re-deriving answers from raw documents on every query. Knowledge accumulates like interest rather than being thrown away after each session.

See [[concepts/LLM Wiki Pattern]] for the full pattern description.

Also the source of [[concepts/Agentic Loops]] (`LOOPS.md`) — field notes on running agent harnesses unsupervised for hours or days: separate planner/generator/evaluator roles, negotiate a testable contract before writing code, persist state to disk instead of context, and delete harness scaffolding as models improve.
