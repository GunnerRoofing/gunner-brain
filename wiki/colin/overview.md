# GunnerCam — Overview

## App

GunnerCam — multi-tenant field-operations web app for roofing crews. Captures and organizes job-site photos and field data on a per-tenant (white-label) basis.

## Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js
- **Cloud:** AWS
- **Architecture:** multi-tenant
- **Photos:** CompanyCam white-label integration
- **Billing:** Stripe
- **Project management:** Monday.com

## Key Integrations

- **GunnerTeam iOS** — customer photos + webhooks (photos captured/synced through the iOS app flow into GunnerCam tenants).

## Status

In active development.
